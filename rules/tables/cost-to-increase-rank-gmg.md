# Cost to Increase Rank  
*Source: Gamemastery Guide p. 199*  

| Rank | Minimum Level | Skill Point Cost |
|------|---------------|------------------|
| Untrained to trained | 1 | 1 |
| Trained to expert | 3 | 2 |
| Expert to master | 7 | 4 |
| Master to legendary | 15 | 8 |
