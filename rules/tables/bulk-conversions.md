# Bulk conversions  
*Source: Core Rulebook p. 295*  

| Creature Size | Bulk Limit | Treats as Light | Treats as Negligible |
|---------------|------------|-----------------|----------------------|
| Tiny | Half | — | none |
| Small or Med. | Standard | L | — |
| Large | ×2 | 1 Bulk | L |
| Huge | ×4 | 2 Bulk | 1 Bulk |
| Gargantuan | ×8 | 4 Bulk | 2 Bulk |
