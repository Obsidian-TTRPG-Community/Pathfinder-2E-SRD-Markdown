# Extend Elixir  *Feat 12*  
[alchemist](rules/traits/alchemist.md "Alchemist Class Trait")  


Integrating your own personal energy into the elixirs you create causes them to affect you for longer. When you consume one of your alchemical items that has the [elixir](rules/traits/elixir.md "Elixir Item Trait") and [infused](rules/traits/infused.md "Infused Item Trait") traits and a duration of 1 minute or longer, that elixir's duration is doubled.
