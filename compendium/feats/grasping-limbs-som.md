# Grasping Limbs  *Feat 12*  
[evolution](rules/traits/evolution-som.md "Evolution Feat Trait")  [summoner](rules/traits/summoner-som.md "Summoner Class Trait")  


Your eidolon grabs enemies. Choose one of the eidolon's unarmed attacks with the [grapple](rules/traits/grapple.md "Grapple Weapon Trait") trait. It gains the [Grab](rules/abilities/grab.md) action on that unarmed attack.

*Source: Secrets of Magic p. 71*
