# Wind Jump  *Feat 10*  
[monk](rules/traits/monk.md "Monk Class Trait")  

- **Prerequisites**: ki spells

You gather the wind beneath you, allowing you to soar as you jump. You gain the [wind jump](compendium/spells/wind-jump.md) ki spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.

*Source: Core Rulebook p. 163*
