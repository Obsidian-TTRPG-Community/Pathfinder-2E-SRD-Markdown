# Invoke Disaster  *Feat 18*  
[druid](rules/traits/druid.md "Druid Class Trait")  

- **Prerequisites**: [Wind Caller](compendium/feats/wind-caller.md)

You can invoke nature's fury upon your foes. You gain the [storm lord](compendium/spells/storm-lord.md) order spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.
