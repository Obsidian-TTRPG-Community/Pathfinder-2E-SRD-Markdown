# Hunter's Vision  *Feat 10*  
[ranger](rules/traits/ranger.md "Ranger Class Trait")  

- **Prerequisites**: warden spells

You are so focused on your hunted prey that you can see it clearly even beyond the limits of your senses. You gain the [hunter's vision](compendium/spells/hunters-vision-apg.md) warden spell. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1.

*Source: Advanced Player's Guide p. 132*
