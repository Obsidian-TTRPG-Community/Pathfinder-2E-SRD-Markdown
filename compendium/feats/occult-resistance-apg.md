# Occult Resistance  *Feat 9*  
[changeling](rules/traits/changeling-b1.md "Changeling Ancestry & Heritage Trait")  

- **Prerequisites**: expert in [Occultism](compendium/skills.md#Occultism)

Your study of occult practices has shown you how to best defend against them. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to all saving throws against [occult](rules/traits/occult.md "Occult Tradition Trait") effects.

*Source: Advanced Player's Guide p. 31*
