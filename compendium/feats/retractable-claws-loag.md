# Retractable Claws  *Feat 1*  
[kitsune](rules/traits/kitsune-loag.md "Kitsune Ancestry & Heritage Trait")  


You gain a claw unarmed attack that deals `1d4` slashing damage. Your claws are in the brawling group and have the [agile](rules/traits/agile.md "Agile Weapon Trait"), [finesse](rules/traits/finesse.md "Finesse Weapon Trait"), and [unarmed](rules/traits/unarmed.md "Unarmed Weapon Trait") traits.

*Source: Lost Omens: Ancestry Guide p. 125*
