# Divine Countermeasures  *Feat 9*  
[aasimar](rules/traits/aasimar-apg.md "Aasimar Ancestry & Heritage Trait")  


You've studied your celestial heritage with the intent of better defending yourself, and you've found that your techniques are equally powerful against celestials, fiends, and other divine entities. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to all saving throws against [divine](rules/traits/divine.md "Divine Tradition Trait") effects.

*Source: Advanced Player's Guide p. 36*
