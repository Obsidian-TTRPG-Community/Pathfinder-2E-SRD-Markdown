# Celestial Strikes  *Feat 13*  
[aasimar](rules/traits/aasimar-apg.md "Aasimar Ancestry & Heritage Trait")  


Your connection to good arms all your attacks against forces of evil. All your weapon and unarmed [Strikes](rules/actions/strike.md) deal 1 additional good damage and have the [good](rules/traits/good.md "Good Alignment Trait") and [magical](rules/traits/magical.md "Magical Item Trait") traits.

*Source: Advanced Player's Guide p. 36*
