# Stone Face  *Feat 1*  
[hobgoblin](rules/traits/hobgoblin-locg.md "Hobgoblin Ancestry & Heritage Trait")  


Through conditioning or experience, you've mastered the art of composure, even in the face of fear. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to saves against effects with the [fear](rules/traits/fear.md "Fear Effect Trait") trait, and a +2 circumstance bonus to your Will DC against Intimidate skill actions, such as [Demoralize](rules/actions/demoralize.md).

*Source: Lost Omens: Ancestry Guide p. 35*
