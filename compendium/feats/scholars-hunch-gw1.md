# Scholar's Hunch  [R](rules/core-rulebook/chapter-9-playing-the-game.md#Actions "Reaction") *Feat 8*

- **Prerequisites**: [Oatia Skysage Dedication](compendium/feats/oatia-skysage-dedication-gw1.md)
- **Frequency**: once per hour
- **Trigger** You fail a [Lore](compendium/skills.md#Lore) or [Occultism](compendium/skills.md#Occultism) skill check.

Scholarly debates have taught you to think on your feet. Reroll the failed check and use the new result.
