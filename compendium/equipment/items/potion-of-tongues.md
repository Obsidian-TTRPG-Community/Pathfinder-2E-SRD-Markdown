# Potion of Tongues *Item 12*  
[consumable](rules/traits/consumable.md "Consumable Item Trait")  [divination](rules/traits/divination.md "Divination School Trait")  [magical](rules/traits/magical.md "Magical Item Trait")  [potion](rules/traits/potion.md "Potion Item Trait")  [uncommon](rules/traits/uncommon.md "Uncommon Rarity Trait")  

- **Price** 320 gp
- **Bulk** L; **Usage** held in 1 hand
- **Activate** [Interact](rules/actions/interact.md)

This sour potion enlivens your tongue with unusual flavors and uncommon eloquence, allowing you to speak and understand all languages for 4 hours after you drink it. This doesn't allow you to read these languages in their written form.
