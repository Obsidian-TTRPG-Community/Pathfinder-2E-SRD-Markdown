# Clockwork Macuahuitl *Item 0*  
[backswing](rules/traits/backswing.md "Backswing Weapon Trait")  [clockwork](rules/traits/clockwork-g-g.md "Clockwork  Trait")  [forceful](rules/traits/forceful.md "Forceful Weapon Trait")  [rare](rules/traits/rare.md "Rare Rarity Trait")  [versatile <b>](rules/traits/versatile-b.md "Versatile Weapon Trait")  

- **Price** 550 gp
- **Bulk** 1
  - **Damage**: `1d10` S
- **Hands** 2
- **Category** Advanced; **Group** Club 

This finely-made wooden club has a beautiful, lacquered finish that gleams in the sunlight. A heavy ring of gears lined with sharpened pieces of obsidian automatically and constantly spin around the bulk of the wooden club lengthwise. Striking a foe digs the obsidian gears into the enemy's flesh and tears it with its blades. The ever-turning gears also help to dislodge an enemy's defensive position against the weapon.

The clockwork macuahuitl deals `1d10` slashing damage and has the [backswing](rules/traits/backswing.md "Backswing Weapon Trait"), [forceful](rules/traits/forceful.md "Forceful Weapon Trait"), and versatile B traits. The clockwork macuahuitl is a two-handed advanced weapon in the club weapon group.
